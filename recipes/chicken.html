Air Fired Chicken

Description

Prep: 5 mins

Cook: 15 mins

Total: 20 mins

Servings: 5

Yield: 5 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chicken wings
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning mix
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Steps

Step 1

Combine chicken wings, taco seasoning, and oil in a resealable plastic bag. Shake to coat.

Step 2

Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 2 minutes.

Step 3

Place wings in the air fryer and cook for 12 minutes, turning after 6 minutes. Serve immediately.
